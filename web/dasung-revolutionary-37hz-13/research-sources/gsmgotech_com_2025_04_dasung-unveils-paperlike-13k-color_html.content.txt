In a bold move poised to redefine the e-ink display market, Dasung has launched the**Paperlike 13K Color**, a groundbreaking portable monitor that combines the eye-friendly benefits of e-ink technology with vibrant colors, a responsive touchscreen, and an unprecedented 37Hz refresh rate. Touted as the world’s first 3K-resolution color e-ink monitor, this device aims to bridge the gap between traditional LCD screens and the growing demand for low-eye-strain alternatives.

### A Leap Forward for E-Ink Technology

E-ink displays have long been celebrated for their paper-like readability and minimal glare, making them ideal for reading, writing, and extended work sessions. However, their limitations—particularly sluggish refresh rates and monochrome displays—have hindered broader adoption. Dasung’s Paperlike 13K Color shatters these barriers with a**13.3-inch 3K (3104 x 4134) color e-ink panel**that supports 37Hz refresh rates, a first for the industry. This leap in performance allows for smoother scrolling, video playback, and even light gaming, all while retaining the signature e-ink comfort.

“We wanted to create a device that doesn’t force users to choose between eye comfort and functionality,” said a Dasung spokesperson. “With 37Hz, colors, and touch responsiveness, the Paperlike 13K Color isn’t just an e-reader—it’s a full-fledged monitor for work and creativity.”

### Key Features That Set It Apart

The Paperlike 13K Color isn’t just about specs; it’s designed for real-world versatility:

*   **37Hz Refresh Rate****:** Eliminates the “ghosting” effect common in e-ink displays, enabling seamless transitions for tasks like coding, graphic design, or watching tutorials.
*   **16-Level Color Display****:**While not as vivid as LCD screens, it offers sufficient color depth for charts, illustrations, and casual media consumption.
*   **Touchscreen & Pen Support****:** A Wacom-style stylus (sold separately) turns the monitor into a digital notebook, ideal for artists and note-takers.
*   **Portability****:**Weighing just 1.1 pounds and powered via USB-C, it’s a travel-friendly companion for laptops, smartphones, or tablets.
*   **Eye Care Modes****:**Adjustable front lights with warm and cool tones reduce blue light exposure for nighttime use.

For professionals battling screen fatigue or digital nomads working in sunlit environments, the Paperlike 13K Color promises to be a game-changer.

[**Learn more or purchase the Dasung Paperlike 13K Color here**](https://shop.dasung.com/products/dasung-paperlike-13k-the-worlds-first-37hz-3k-e-ink-monitor).

### Who Is It For?

The monitor caters to a niche yet growing audience:

*   **Writers & Researchers****:** Marathon typing sessions without eye strain.
*   **Digital Artists****:** Sketch and edit with e-ink’s natural texture.
*   **Outdoor Workers****:**Sunlight-readable screens for construction plans or fieldwork.
*   **Gamers?:**While 37Hz won’t rival high-end gaming monitors, Dasung hints at “surprising compatibility” with turn-based games like chess or visual novels.

### Market Impact and Availability

Priced at $1,999 (early bird discounts available), the Paperlike 13K Color is a premium product, but its specs justify the cost for its target audience. Competitors like Onyx Boox and reMarkable dominate the e-ink tablet space, but Dasung’s focus on high-speed color monitors carves out a unique niche. Early adopters praise its balance of innovation and practicality, though some note the color palette remains muted compared to LCDs.

As remote work and digital detox trends surge, Dasung’s latest release could signal a new era for e-ink—one where functionality finally matches its famed comfort. Whether you’re a programmer, artist, or simply someone who values eye health, the Paperlike 13K Color invites you to rethink what a monitor can be.
