Dasung Paperlike 13K Revolutionary Portable Monitor With 37Hz refresh Rate Launched
===================================================================================

[Dasung](https://goodereader.com/blog/e-paper/dasung-paperlike-13k-revolutionary-portable-monitor-with-37hz-refresh-rate-launched#) announced a 13.3-inch E Ink [monitor](https://goodereader.com/blog/e-paper/dasung-paperlike-13k-revolutionary-portable-monitor-with-37hz-refresh-rate-launched#) with 300 PPI and a 37Hz ultra-high refresh rate.

[![Image 9: - Good e-Reader](https://media.goodereader.com/blog/uploads/images/2025/03/02041012/dasung-paperlike-revolutionary-monitor.jpg.webp)](https://media.goodereader.com/blog/uploads/images/2025/03/02041012/dasung-paperlike-revolutionary-monitor.jpg)

Dasung has announced the launch of a new 13.3-inch E Ink monitor with 300 PPI resolution and a 37Hz God-Like ultra-high refresh rate. The monochrome e-paper display with a resolution of 3200 x 2400 pixels also benefits from Auto-Clear automatic image clearing technology, which ensures quick and effective removal of the remnants of the previous display. This way, you have a clean and fresh new display every time.

The monitor otherwise continues with the same basic design layout, which includes a thicker portion along the base that tapers off to get thinner towards the top. The thicker portion is also where the ports are positioned, which include a Mini-HDMI video interface, a Type-C interface, and a 3.5mm headphone jack. The thicker portion also hosts the DC power port and the speaker + 3.5 mm headphone slot on either side.

The monitor measures 307 x 251 x 5 mm at its thinnest portion. Further, the monitor also boasts a strong build, thanks to its aluminum alloy construction. It can connect to both a smartphone and a [computer](https://goodereader.com/blog/e-paper/dasung-paperlike-13k-revolutionary-portable-monitor-with-37hz-refresh-rate-launched#). For this, the monitor comes bundled with an HDMI to Mini-HDMI as well as a Type-C to Type-C cable to help you get started right away. This way, you can have a secondary eye-friendly display where you can do most of your reading.

The Dasung 13.3-inch Paperlike 13K Revolutionary portable monitor can be ordered via [JingDong](https://u.jd.com/OOTXsvx) where it is retailing for 4,489 yuan. That comes to around 618 USD as per current conversion rates.
