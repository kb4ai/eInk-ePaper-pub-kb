- **Screen:** 25.3" E Ink Carta display
- **Resolution:** 3200 x 1800 pixels (145 PPI)
- **Dimensions:** 594.2 × 349.2 × 26.5 mm
- **Weight:** 2,750g (monitor), 2,730g (stand)
- **Construction:** Aluminum alloy body

- **USB Type-C:** 1× USB-C port
